This project is about a flying bird which is keeping descend due to gravity and the player must control the mouse to prevent it from colliding with walls or obstacles.The game requires precise timings and quick reactions because people cannot press the key either too early or too late.
As the game goes on, the game can be harder by making the walls appear more frequently or by increasing the falling speed of birds
It’s interesting because it’s easy to understand and hard to master. People need to make quick decisions under the pressure of fast falling and appearing obstacles. The simple controls, increasing difficulty and instant feedback make the game addictive and enjoyable. 
In the initial version of this project, we may not add the UI but just test the colliding part of birds and obstacles. There will also be the system of score. The drawing of birds and obstacles will be added after this.
